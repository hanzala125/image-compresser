Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Yes! All compression happens in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy and security.

What file types are supported?

We support JPG, PNG, GIF, WEBP for images, PDF documents, and MP4, MOV, AVI, WEBM for videos.

Is there a file size limit?

Yes, we limit images to 20MB, PDFs to 50MB, and videos to 100MB to ensure fast processing in your browser.

How much can I reduce my file sizes?

Compression results vary, but typically you can reduce image sizes by 50-80%, PDFs by 30-70%, and videos by 40-90% depending on settings.
